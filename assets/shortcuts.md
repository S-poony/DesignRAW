## Shortcuts and controls

### Global

`ctrl + z` = undo
`ctrl + y` = redo
`ctrl + shift + z` = redo

### Rectangle

`click` = split
`ctrl + click` = delete
`alt + click` = alternate split 

***

### Image

`click` = change image fit *(cover or contain)*
`shift + click` = split to Target A *(left or top)*
`ctrl + shift + click` = split to Target B *(right or bottom)*
